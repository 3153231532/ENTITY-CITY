How to Pay

To complete your payment, follow these simple steps:

1. Dial the USSD code for your network provider:
MTN: Dial *165#
Airtel: Dial *185#

2. Follow the on-screen prompts to select "Pay Bill" or "Make Payment" and enter the amount.

3. Once the payment request is confirmed, contact our Marketing Manager at: +256 744 843511 for verification.

4. Provide the transaction details (payment reference and amount) to the Marketing Manager.

5. After verification, you will receive a confirmation message and proceed with your purchase.

Available Payment Methods

1. MTN USSD (Dial *165#)

2. Airtel USSD (Dial *185#)

3. Bank Transfers (To be arranged with the Marketing Manager)
