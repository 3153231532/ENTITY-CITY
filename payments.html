Payment Methods

At Entity City, we offer a convenient and secure payment system through USSD for MTN and Airtel users. Payments are processed directly by communicating with our Marketing Manager to complete the transaction. This ensures that your purchase is handled promptly and securely.

1. How to Make a Payment

To complete your payment, follow these simple steps:

  • Dial the USSD code for MTN or Airtel, depending on your network:
    • MTN: Dial *165#
    • Airtel: Dial *185#
  • Follow the on-screen prompts to select "Pay Bill" or "Make Payment" and enter the amount.
  • Once the payment request is confirmed, reach out to our Marketing Manager via phone:
    • Contact: +256 744 843511
  • Provide the transaction details, including the payment reference and amount, to the Marketing Manager for verification.
  • Once the payment is verified, you will receive a confirmation message and proceed with your purchase.

2. Available Payment Methods

Payments can be made through the following methods:

  • MTN USSD (Dial *165#)
  • Airtel USSD (Dial *185#)
  • Bank Transfers (To be arranged with the Marketing Manager)

3. Payment Security

We take your payment security seriously. All USSD transactions are processed using encrypted connections to ensure that your personal and financial details are secure. Our Marketing Manager also verifies each payment to guarantee that transactions are legitimate.

4. Refunds and Disputes

In case of any payment disputes or refund requests, please contact our Marketing Manager as soon as possible at +256 744 843511. We will work with you to resolve any issues quickly and ensure your satisfaction.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about the payment process, or if you need assistance, please feel free to reach out to our Marketing Manager at:
