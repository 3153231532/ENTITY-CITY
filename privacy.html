Our Privacy Policy

At Entity City, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your information is safe with us. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details when you make a purchase, sign up for newsletters, or engage with our services. Additionally, we may collect information about your browsing behavior and preferences using cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide, improve, and personalize our services to you. This includes processing your orders, sending promotional emails, improving the user experience, and analyzing trends to better understand our customers’ needs.

3. Data Protection and Security

We use advanced security protocols to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or misuse. All payment transactions are encrypted using secure methods, and we follow industry best practices to ensure your data is safe with us.

4. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, or delivering goods. These partners are obligated to keep your information confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. If you would like to update your preferences or request that we delete your information, please contact us at support@entitycity.com.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These small files help us remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and improve our services. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
